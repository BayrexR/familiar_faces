Abstract

The Familiar Faces Project takes a dive into the world of machine learning and facial recognition models. The purpose is to evaluate the accuracy of a predictive face detection algorithm trained on facial features to detect human faces, gender, and the following emotions.

-Afraid -Angry -Happy -Disgusted -Neutral -Sad -Surprised

Hypothesis

Testing the accuracy of this facial recognition model in detecting emotions and gender could open the door to a lot of future real-world applications. We believe, that with sufficiently large training samples, face recognition algorithms could be applied in many different markets.
